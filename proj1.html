KT Y Branded Content

Gen Z Content Creation & Brand Communication for YouTube & Instagram

Date

Mar – Jul 2025

Role

Content Creator & Marketer

Tools

Premiere Pro, Instagram for Business, YouTube Analytics

Category

Branded Content, Social Media

Overview

I produced branded content for KT's Gen Z brand, 'Y' across YouTube and Instagram. All videos were published, with Instagram Reels reaching up to 121K views, and YouTube videos consistently achieving 3K+ views per upload.

Beyond production, I played a key coordination role with in-house practitioners. I shared video briefs and PPMs, collected feedback via messenger comments, and revised scripts and on-screen copy—especially for an event-focused video where communicating accurate event details was critical.

Challenge

KT needed to strengthen awareness and relevance for its Gen Z brand in a saturated telecom market. The goal was to create content that felt native to each platform—entertaining and relatable first, while still delivering clear brand value.

Key challenges included:

  • Gen Z topics and formats that naturally earn attention on YouTube and Instagram
  • Avoiding 'corporate ad' tone while keeping the Y brand message intact
  • Ensuring accuracy for time-sensitive event information without sacrificing watchability
  • Proving impact through measurable performance

Strategy

Audience & Trend Research

I researched Gen Z interests and platform trends to define content topics that feel familiar and shareable, such as campus life, dating spots, and lifestyle. I also studied short-form hooks, pacing, and caption styles that improve early retention.

Branded-Content Approach

To keep the content authentic, I connected the Y brand message to real-life situations instead of forcing product-forward messaging. For the event-related video targeting Gen Z students living alone, I focused on everyday 'living alone' moments and then delivered the event information clearly and accurately.

Execution

Branded Content Production

  • Planned and produced branded videos for YouTube and Instagram for KT's Gen Z brand 'Y'
  • Created video briefs and PPMs to align on concept, storyline, and deliverables
  • Executed production tasks including filming, editing, and thumbnail creation
  • Produced an event promo video targeting Gen Z students living alone, translating the brand's value into a relatable, platform-native narrative

In-house Practitioners Communication & Feedback

  • Coordinated directly with KT practitioners by sharing briefs and receiving messenger comments
  • Refined scripts, captions, and on-screen text based on feedback to ensure brand alignment
  • For the event promo, prioritized accuracy of event details (key messages, required wording, and information clarity) before publishing

Performance Tracking

  • Tracked key metrics such as views and overall content performance after publishing
  • Used learnings to improve pacing, messaging clarity, and topic selection for future content
