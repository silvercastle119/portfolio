KT Telecom Service Proposal

Creator Segment Strategy, Qualitative Research, and Feasibility-Driven Marketing Proposal

Date

Mar – May 2025

Role

Research & Strategy

Tools

Google Slides, Figma, Desk Research, Interviews

Category

Segmentation, Marketing Strategy, Service Proposal

Overview

I proposed a creator-focused segment strategy to strengthen KT’s competitiveness in the telecom market. I synthesized desk research and in-depth creator interviews into clear recommendations, connecting audience insights to offer and messaging decisions and translating them into an execution-ready direction.

The project received Second Prize, with feedback noting that we identified a previously overlooked segment and presented it persuasively.

Challenge

The key challenge was to define a new growth segment and communicate it in a way that felt relevant, credible, and actionable, especially in a crowded telecom market.

Strategy

Audience insight and segment definition

I mapped the creator work journey and validated it through qualitative interviews to define creators as a high-value customer group for telecom.

Execution-ready recommendations

I interviewed creators with 100K+ subscribers to capture needs, barriers, and workflows, then translated the findings into feasible service and promotion directions.

Process

01

Research

Desk research on telecom market context and creator ecosystem, plus competitor and category reference review

02

Insight

Mapped the creator work journey and conducted qualitative interviews to validate needs, barriers, and value drivers

03

Strategy

Defined the creator segment and developed service and promotion directions, with feasibility grounded in KT precedents

04

Alignment

Refined the deck and execution scenario by coordinating with in-house practitioners, then finalized script and presentation

Execution

  • Conducted desk research to clarify KT’s market context and identify opportunity areas for growth.
  • interviewed creators with 100K+ subscribers to capture qualitative insights on needs, barriers, and workflows.
  • Defined the creator segment and developed service and promotion directions, including messaging and creative approach.
  • Coordinated with in-house practitioners to incorporate feedback and refine an execution scenario grounded in KT’s existing service precedents, improving feasibility.
  • Built the presentation deck and mockups, wrote the script, and delivered the final pitch.
