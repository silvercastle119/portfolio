Bar & Pub Revitalization

Gen Z Insight, Participation-Driven Activation, and Budgeted Execution

Date

Sep – Dec 2022

Role

Project Lead & Planner

Tools

Desk Research, Interviews, Microsoft Presentation, Budget Planning

Category

Local Marketing, Brand Repositioning, Experiential Activation

Overview

Juyuso is a bar and pub, and this project focused on rebuilding the relationship between the venue and Gen Z customers after COVID-19 by repositioning the concept and executing a participation-driven activation.

After we were selected as the 1st-place pitch team, we moved beyond a proposal and delivered the project in-market. I defined the problem through Gen Z behavior and consumption insights, designed creative, and ensured feasibility through budgeting and execution planning. Based on the owner’s feedback, Gen Z visits and sales increased by about 20%.

Challenge

The challenge was to translate Juyuso’s strengths into a simple value proposition and connect it to an experience that could drive real visits through participation.

Strategy

Gen Z insight-led reframing

I analyzed Gen Z consumption trends and clarified the behavioral shift toward smaller-group occasions and taste-driven choices, then reframed the problem as a positioning and experience gap.

Feasibility-first planning

I built a budget plan, prioritized spend items, and aligned the event scope to what could realistically be operated.

Process

01

Research

Analyzed post-COVID Gen Z drinking and consumption trends and reviewed local nightlife competitors to frame what drives real visits.

02

Insight

Clarified the behavioral shift and defined the core problem through Gen Z behavior and consumption insights, translating it into a participation-led experience brief.

03

Creative

Planned immersive creative and designed a simple, repeatable participation mechanic that connects the brand to an on-site experience.

04

Execution

Built a budget plan, prioritized spend items, aligned the event scope, and led project coordination from setup to in-market delivery.
